Dragon Pit

Dragon Pit is an NFT based passive Play-to-Earn card game built on Metis.   Owners of BTE Dragons are automatically enrolling their majestic creatures to fight other dragons by minting/buying them.     

Each week a war takes place where teams are fighting in a battle daily in randomized order. The first 4 days are the qualifier rounds, the 5th and 6th are semi finals and finally the last day is the final battle.

  In a battle each minted dragon of the fighting teams are paired with the enemy’s creatures randomly in 1 to 1 fight and using Attack, Defense, HP and Luck attributes ending up as winner and looser. The team who have more 1-to-1 wins defeats the enemy and can continue the war (day 5-6-7) The winner team gets reward in Metis each day and the final winner of the war received extra bonus at the end of the week.  

Rewards

Rewards are calculated based on the previous day’s transactions of the BTE Dragon cards. 

Each NFT sale’s royalty fee contributes the treasury. 

The winner team of daily battle receives 60% of the previous day’s treasury. The winner of the war receives the rest 40% of the treasury of each day of the week on the top of the daily reward   The daily reward is shared within the winner team based on the number of NFTs a user holds and the 1-to-1 wins of those dragons:    
70% of the daily reward shared between  the winner NFT holders and 30% between the losers.    

The bonus of the final winner team (40% of each day’s treasury) of the war is shared equally regardless of the individual Dragon won a fight or not.
